DooM

A wasm32 port of 1997 linuxdoom-1.10 with minimal modifications to the sources.

Use enter to start the game, arrow keys to move, ctrl to shoot, spacebar to open gates, alt and arrow keys to strafe (if your browser does not handle these keys otherwise). No mobile touch input supported right now.
